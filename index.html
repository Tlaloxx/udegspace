ABOUT

We promote space science in young students

Udeg Space is a university community founded by students from the "Universidad de Guadalajara" whose main interest is the development and disclosure of space sciences in México.

"Our mission is to promote talent through multidisciplinary technology projects to build a better future for all."

Tlaloc Álvarez - Co-founder

Project-based learning

1Work in team
Multidisciplinary projects allow learning to collaborate as a team, creating useful skills for the student's professional life.
2Great Strategic projects Planning
The success of a project depends to a great extent on correct planning. With our projects, students acquire management and planning skills very early in their professional life.
3Motivate students to learn
Putting the acquired technical knowledge into practice reinforces the student's interest in continuing to learn.
